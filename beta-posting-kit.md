## Reddit: r/alphaandbetausers

### Titulo

`Looking for beta testers for an Android-first app for people with irregular income`

### Cuerpo

`Hi, I'm testing a mobile-first app called HoySi. It's for people who get paid in parts, sell through WhatsApp, or mix household and business money.`

`I'm not looking for generic praise. I need 5 minutes of real use and one concrete note about what felt confusing, what's missing, or what actually helped.`

`Good fit if you:`
- `use Android`
- `have irregular income`
- `sell, collect payments, or manage cash flow in short sessions`

`What I'd love you to do:`
- `open the app`
- `use it with your real situation`
- `leave 1 short feedback note after`

`Beta link: https://hoysi.onrender.com/launch.html?source=reddit-alpha`
